常用公式证明

\$3^{\log_2n} = n^{\log_23}\$

证明:

欲证\$3^{\log_2n} = n^{\log_23}\$, 只需证\$\log_2(3^{\log_2n}) = \log_2(n^{\log_23})\$
$$ \begin{align*} \because & & \log_2(3^{\log_2n}) = \log_2n \cdot \log_23 \\ & & \log_2(n^{\log_23}) = \log_23 \cdot \log_2n \\ \therefore & & \log_2(3^{\log_2n}) = \log_2(n^{\log_23}) \\ \end{align*} $$ 证毕
